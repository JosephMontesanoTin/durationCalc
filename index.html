What does Duration Score mean?

  • The Duration score rates a test on how quickly a 5% uplift can be detected between 2-8 weeks.
  • The higher the score, the quicker a test will likely conclude (if it is successful)
  • By setting a standard uplift to look at in a standarized time frame, KPI's can be easily compared. This allows users to prioritize faster wins, for more immediate uplifts.
